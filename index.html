Coaching for Leaders & Teams

Targeted and Impactful

As both business and personal life get more complicated and fulfillment seemingly becomes further away, the need for individualized attention becomes a necessity. Coaching is a strategy that provides a customized approach to your personal and professional development.

Dr. Dan Neundorf’s coaching approach is governed by these 6 Principles:

  • Principle 1: Clarity in Fundamentals — Aligning mission, vision, strategy, competencies and goals with personal and professional success.
  • Principle 2: Differentiating Delivery — Delivering targeted coaching services that lead to profound learning and higher levels of performance.
  • Principle 3: Tenacious Follow Through — Ensuring long-term sustainability, value and greater return on personal investment.
  • Principle 4: The Human Touch — Engaging in innovative interactions that lead to lasting change.
  • Principle 5: Positivity — Establishing the mindset of client success in all domains.
  • Principle 6: Focused Action — Translating learning into activities that increase performance and value to the individual and organization.

Coaching Services

One-on-one Coaching

I work with you one-on-one to help navigate your business challenges and mine for opportunities that require a strategic and at times a non-linear approach. I can support you in a wide array of competency areas. Please contact me for more details.

Group Coaching

I work with groups where the goal of the entire group is to advance in an area that supports the entire group. Group coaching uses the intellectual capital of the group and my guidance to work through complex challenges, develop skills or knowledge and enhance the performance of the group. Please contact me for more details.

Just-in-time Coaching

Perhaps you have a one-time challenge or opportunity that you need to discuss with me immediately. Maybe you have a critical meeting coming up, a crucial conversation, an important decision… just in time coaching will provide you with what you need to move forward successfully. Please contact me for more details.
